## What Data We Collect

When you use Orbit, we collect:

- **Account information** — your email address and encrypted password (via Supabase Auth)
- **Content you create** — messages, tasks, events, ideas, and reminders you type into the app
- **Images you upload** — photos you choose to add to your memory lane
- **Usage data** — basic app activity (e.g. messages sent per day) stored locally on your device

We do **not** collect your name unless you voluntarily enter it during onboarding. We do **not** collect location, contacts, or any device information.

---

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

1. Provide the app's features (organising your notes, displaying your history)
2. Process your messages through OpenAI's API to extract tasks, events, ideas, and reminders
3. Store your content so you can access it across sessions

---

## Third-Party Services

Orbit uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Supabase | Database and authentication | [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy) |
| OpenAI | AI extraction of tasks/ideas from your messages | [openai.com/privacy](https://openai.com/privacy) |

Your message content is sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI does not use API data to train their models by default. See [OpenAI's API data usage policy](https://openai.com/policies/api-data-usage) for details.

---

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on Supabase servers (hosted on AWS)
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Data is stored per user and inaccessible to other users
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes
